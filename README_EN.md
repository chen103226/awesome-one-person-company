<summary><b>ğŸ’° Business Model Selection</b></summary>

**6 Best Business Models for One Person Companies:**

1. **Micro SaaS** ($1K-100K ARR)

   - Example: Plausible ($1M ARR, 3-person team)
   - Pros: Predictable revenue | High automation
   - Challenges: Need continuous marketing | Customer churn
2. **Paid Newsletter** ($5K-500K ARR)

   - Example: Stratechery ($3M ARR, 1 person)
   - Pros: Low cost | Direct relationship
   - Challenges: Need continuous output | Build personal brand
3. **Digital Products** ($10K-1M ARR)

   - Example: Courses/templates on Gumroad
   - Pros: Create once, sell many times
   - Challenges: Need marketing | Piracy risk
4. **API Services** ($10K-1M ARR)

   - Example: Bannerbear ($600K ARR)
   - Pros: B2B high ASP | Easy integration
   - Challenges: High technical barrier
5. **Open Source + Sponsorship** ($5K-100K ARR)

   - Example: Little Panda C++ (50K RMB/month)
   - Pros: China specialty | Community support
   - Challenges: Unstable revenue
6. **Consulting + Productization** ($50K-500K ARR)

   - Example: Domain expert consultant
   - Pros: Quick monetization | High profit margin
   - Challenges: Time for money | Hard to scale

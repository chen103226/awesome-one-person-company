# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this list. This project is a curated resource for one-person companies, and quality matters.

## What we accept

- People with real products and visible proof of work
- Books, essays, and long-form interviews from primary sources
- Tools and communities with clear value to solo founders
- Chinese resources with authoritative sources

## What we avoid

- Get-rich-quick schemes or growth hacks
- Low-quality aggregators or affiliate spam
- Unsupported claims without sources

## How to contribute

1. Fork the repo and create a new branch.
2. Add your entry in the right section of `README.md` or `README_CN.md`.
3. Use the following format:

```md

```

4. Keep entries concise and consistent.
5. Add sources from official sites, author pages, or long-form interviews.
6. Open a pull request with a short explanation.

## Link rules

- Prefer official sites or author pages
- One primary link per entry is enough
- Avoid shortened or tracking links

## Style

- Keep sentences short
- No marketing claims
- Be factual and verifiable
